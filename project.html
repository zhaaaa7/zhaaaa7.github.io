MyMemory App

MyMemory is an app that aims at helping users recording their life. We hope that the user can see this app as a private photo management tool with which they can develop the habit of timely managing their photos so as to lose their valuable memory. We also incorporate e-commerce element into the app where users can buy physical albums.
