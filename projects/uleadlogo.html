Logo Design for
ULead

(ULead: University Leaders for Educational Access and DiversityNetwork)

Background

The uLEAD (University Leaders for Educational Access and Diversity) Network is an online community of university leaders committed to broadening postsecondary access and support for all students, regardless of immigration status. University leaders encompass a wide variety of individuals in the field, including: presidents, board members, diversity officers, admissions officers, financial aid officers, registrars, student affairs professionals, general counsel, and student activists.

Color Palette

The color I choose is blue and mazie, but brighter than UM colors. Brighter colors feels more lively and convey a feeling of hope, which means the life of undocumented students’ life will be more promising with the help of uLEAD.



Typography

The typeface I choose is Helvetica because the space between its x-height is tall which makes the words easily recongized even in small size.

Concept Explanation

The “E” letter is larger than others because uLEAD devotes to provide education for undocumented, which is the most important message the logo should convey.

Also, the “D” letter stands for “diversity” is another message that needs to be emphasized in this logo. The “D”, consisted with circles, is to show the embrace of diversity.
