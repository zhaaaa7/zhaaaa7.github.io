CMS
Content management system such as Drupal and Wordpress give us security and consistency support where we just need to focus our attention on the centent. However, working with frameworks means working with uncontrollable risks.

I worked with drupal for about four months and even wrote my own modules to alter the default core function. I was often frustrated when error occurs because I was unable to trace the root. This is not good feeling for a developer, who is often eagered to learn how things works under the hood.

Frameworks make easy things easier, and make hard things impossible.
