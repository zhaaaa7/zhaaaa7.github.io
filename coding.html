HTML is the foundation of Accessibility. I really didn't understand it's importance until I used Apple Voice Over to navigate my website in one class exercise. Though accessibility is more emphasized in academic world, using semantic HTML should be a concern in real industry.

CMS
Content management system such as Drupal and Wordpress provide us with security and consistency support. However, working with frameworks means working with uncontrollable risks because we don't know how things work under the hood.

I worked with drupal for about four months and even wrote my own modules to alter the default Drupal core functionality. I was often frustrated when error occurs because I was unable to trace the root.

With frameworks, you'd better be satisfied with just knowing how things work. Frameworks make easy things easier, and make hard things impossible.
