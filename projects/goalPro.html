Main Features

Plan sharing

Users can find many people who shares the same goal with them and follow thier plans.

Measurement

We provide a set of professional measurement methods for all differnet kinds of goals.   

Data Viz

Users' progress towards their goals will be tracked in the form of various visualizations. 

Notification

Users can set up notifications every day which helps to ensure the success of the plan. 

Behavior Theory

The features of final project are all based on the five stages of behavior change: pre-contemplation, contemplation, preparation, action and maintenance.

We also refer to the Elephant and Rider theory, trying to direct the rider and motivate the elephant at the same time. The plans from other users in the community and the measurement methods library help Rider make right decisions. And the different kinds of visualization of measurement and progress motivates the Elephant.

Also, we add game elements to visualization to create a whole story for users, making them more engaged in the whole process.
